Quartz

Quartz is a hard, crystalline mineral composed of silicon and oxygen atoms. It is one of the famous and most abundant mineral found on earth. In its purest form Quartz is white in colour but different impurities can cause the colour to change to purple, pink, brown etc.

There are multiple varieties of Quartz with different uses. It has a very high melting point and can withstand critically high temperatures. Engineered Quart Stone which are industrially made from raw Quartz are popularly used as countertops and slabs in may residential and commercial buildings. They have certain qualities that make them special. The industry, in fact, is increasingly using the term engineered stone to refer to this type of countertop.

People might think of Quartz in terms of kitchen and bathroom counters. But Quartz is also slabbed out in massive sizes for floors, especially in commercial structures like shopping malls. In addition Quartz does not scratch easily.
