SPC

SPC flooring stone refers to a type of flooring material made from a rigid core composed of stone polymer composite (SPC) that is layered with a high-definition print film and a protective wear layer. This type of flooring is becoming increasingly popular due to its durability, ease of maintenance, and wide range of design options.

The SPC core is a mixture of limestone, PVC, and other stabilizers, making it highly resistant to impact, moisture, and heat. The high-definition print film layer is applied to the surface of the SPC core and can mimic the appearance of a variety of materials, including natural stone, ceramic tiles, and hardwood.

The protective wear layer is applied on top of the print film and helps to resist scratches, stains, and other types of damage. This layer is often treated with special coatings that make the flooring more resistant to UV radiation, making it ideal for use in areas with direct sunlight exposure.

SPC flooring stone is available in a variety of styles, colors, and textures, making it a versatile option for a range of interior design styles. It is also relatively easy to install, often requiring only a simple click-and-lock system or adhesive to secure the planks or tiles in place.

In summary, SPC flooring stone is a durable, low-maintenance flooring option that is ideal for high-traffic areas and spaces where moisture and heat are a concern. With its wide range of design options and ease of installation, it has become a popular choice for both residential and commercial applications.
