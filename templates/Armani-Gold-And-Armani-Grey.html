Porcelain Slabs

MPG Stone is one of the leading Porcelain tile slab manufacturers in India and exports the slabs to the UK, USA, and other countries. The slabs are significantly more cost effective than natural stone slabs and tile. The uniqueness of Porcelain slab is that it can be manufactured with comparable beauty, thanks to inkjet production technologies.

Porcelain slabs are lightweight, durable, nonporous, stain resistant, heat resistant, so they are suitable for floors, accent walls, backsplashes, countertops, and other residential and commercial projects in the area such as kitchen and bathrooms.

Due to its lightweight they are suitable for a variety of design projects, including new installations and remodelling projects.

A Porcelain countertop can handle hot pans and pots placed on its surface. The Porcelain won’t smoke, burn, or emit any harmful substance. They are also UV-resistant. As Porcelain is a good conductor of heat they can make your home feel warm during winters. The other characteristics of Porcelain is that they don’t contract or expand with the changes in temperature, hence they do not crack easily.

Porcelain can be categorised into some of the following types i.e. Homogeneous, Full Coloured-Body, Speckled-Body & Double Loading, and Off-white Body.
